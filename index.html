§ 01 · The qualifier

If you’re here, you already know the math feels impossible.

Rent or a mortgage that doesn’t budge. Groceries that cost more every month. Debt payments that don’t quit. A retirement account that hasn’t seen a contribution in too long. An emergency fund built and rebuilt. All on one paycheck — or two that don’t stretch far enough.

You’ve read the books. You know the principles. What you’re missing isn’t discipline — it’s a coach who understands that your situation deserves a real strategy, not a guilt trip about lifestyle creep.

§ 02 · The process

Three steps. No surprises.

Discovery

A free 30-minute call. We see if we’re a fit and scope the work. No pitch, no pressure.

Blueprint

Two to four weeks of focused work, ending with your One-Page Blueprint and the supporting analysis behind it.

Implement & review

We put the blueprint into action together. Ongoing engagement if it serves you; one-time project if that’s the better fit.
